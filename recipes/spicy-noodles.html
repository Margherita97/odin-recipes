Spicy noodles

About spicy noodles

Spiciy noodles is my go to recipe when I'm craving something tasty and healty, but I have little time (or will) to cook.

In today's recipe I will show you how to make the most basic spicy noodles recipe, by using only vegetables. However, feel free to add whatever other ingredients you would like! Some ideas would be eggs, tofu, chicken...

Ingredients (2 servings):

  • Udon 200 g (I chose udon as they're my favorite type, but you can choose any type of noodles)
  • Carrot 1
  • Champignon mushrooms 4
  • Pepper 1/2
  • Zucchini 1/2
  • Lemon juice 1/2 lemon
  • Siracha sauce 1 tbsp
  • Onion 1/2
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Ginger 1 small piece
  • Soy sauce 2 tbsp
  • Seeds oil 2 tbsp

As finisher:

  • Siracha sauce
  • Sesame seeds
  • Sesame oil

Directions

  1. Cut the vegetables in slices.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan, and then add onion, garlic and ginger.
  3. Once they start to become golden, add the vegetables and stir-fry for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the lemon juice.
  5. Once the lemon juice has evaporated, add the udon, stir them and add some hot water.
  6. Let it cook for 2/3 minutes, then add the soy and siracha sauces.
  7. Your spicy noodles are ready! Serve them and garnish them with some siracha sauce, sesame seeds, and some drops of sesame oil. Enjoy!
